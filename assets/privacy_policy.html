ScanVault Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2024

ScanVault ("the app," "we," "us") is designed with privacy as a core principle. This policy explains what data we access, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All scan history is stored exclusively on your device in encrypted local storage. We have no servers, no databases, and no accounts.

2. Permissions Used

  • Camera: Used solely to scan QR codes and barcodes. No images or video are recorded, stored, or transmitted at any time.
  • Storage / Media: Used only when you explicitly tap "Save to Gallery" to export a generated QR code image that you created.
  • Vibration: Used for haptic feedback when a code is detected.

3. Third Parties

This app contains no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks. We do not integrate with Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Facebook SDK, or any similar service.

4. Local Data Storage

Scan history is stored in a local Hive database on your device. You can delete individual scans or clear the entire history at any time from the History or Settings screens. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data.

5. Children

ScanVault does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. No personal information is collected from any user regardless of age.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected in the app update release notes and on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

7. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at: privacy@scanvault.app
